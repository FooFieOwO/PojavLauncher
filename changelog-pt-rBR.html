▶ 1.18: It's Here

Since 21w37a, Mojang bumped the resource pack version to 8, which rendered the current assets-v0 patch unusable. Este é o patch para essas versões. Simply replace it to the existing assets-v0, and you’re good to go!
A small note: When the popup about 1.17 appears, tap Play Anyway. Apertar em OK irá sobrescrever a atualização. Another small note: 1.17.1 or below will simply not work with this patch.

Here's how to get it to work. Download this JRE17 file according to your CPU architecture. arm for 32bit, arm64 for 64bit, and x86_64 for Chromebooks/WSA.
Regardless of your platform, you'll need to download this resource pack.
